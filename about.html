Our story — small beginnings, big community

Swapnest began as a student side-project in late 2023. We were tired of paying full price for things we used once, and we saw neighbours struggling to exchange items safely. A simple idea — a trusted local swap board — grew quickly.

Mission

To make local swapping safe, simple, and social. We want Swapnest to help communities reduce waste, save money, and build trust one swap at a time.

Values

  • Trust: verification, clear communication, and ratings
  • Simplicity: easy posting and searching
  • Local-first: connecting neighbours, not far-away marketplaces

Meet the team
